About Me

I’m Mick—a sales guy, not an engineer. In May 2025 I discovered research on 40 Hz “gamma” stimulation, connected it to my mom’s story, and set out to build a simple, low‑cost, no‑ads app that could help millions. Thanks to AI, I could turn an idea into reality.

The spark

My mom was one of the sharpest people I’ve ever known. She graduated from Tsinghua University as an engineer, built a successful global manufacturing business, and lived with incredible clarity and determination. In her final months, she didn’t recognize her own family. That loss changed me.

When I read about 40 Hz light and sound in May 2025, it resonated. I looked around and saw purpose‑built machines costing thousands—and apps that were expensive, cluttered, or ad‑heavy. I wanted a different path: a gentle app that’s simple, ad‑free, and extremely affordable.

Built entirely with AI, start to finish

Two years earlier, I wouldn’t have stood a chance. I had zero development experience. But with today’s AI, I moved from idea to prototype, then iterated through testing, refinement, and even this website and marketing copy—guided by AI.

  • Ideation: scope, safety language, and simple UX.
  • Prototyping: generating Swift code, debugging, and tuning the 40 Hz experience.
  • Refinement: accessibility, background audio, timers, colors, device handling.
  • Website & content: structure, copy, assets, SEO basics.

No investors, no team—just persistence, AI assistance, and a mission that mattered.

What matters most

  • Access: low cost, no ads, and a calm interface.
  • Integrity: LucidWave is not a medical device—it’s a wellness tool inspired by research.
  • Giving back: 100% of profits go to brain research and care.
