General

Is LucidWave a medical device?

No. LucidWave is a wellness app inspired by neuroscience. It does not diagnose, treat, cure, or prevent any disease.

Why 40 Hz?

Emerging research suggests that rhythmic 40 Hz (gamma) light and sound may help support memory, attention, and overall cognitive wellness. LucidWave brings this rhythm into a simple daily practice.

How often should I use LucidWave?

Consistency matters—short, regular sessions are recommended. Many users start with 10–20 minutes a day and adjust based on comfort.

Safety

Is it safe for everyone?

If you have photosensitive epilepsy or sensitivity to flicker, avoid light mode and consult a clinician. You can use sound‑only mode instead.

What if I get eye strain or discomfort?

Lower brightness, switch to sound‑only, shorten sessions, or stop using the app. Discontinue if discomfort persists.

Using the App

Does the app work in the background?

Yes — the sound can continue while you lock the screen. Light mode requires the screen to remain on.

Can I change colors and brightness?

Yes. Choose a light color, adjust brightness, and set timers (5, 15, 30 minutes, or custom).
