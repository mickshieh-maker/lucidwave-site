A gentle way to care for the mind

LucidWave is a simple app for 40 Hz light & sound sessions—created in honor of my mom, who was the sharpest person I knew. Toward the end, she didn’t recognize her own family. This is my small way to help—yours, too.

Why 40 Hz?

Early research suggests gamma-frequency (around 40 Hz) stimulation may support brain health. LucidWave offers an easy way to run light and sound sessions—adjust brightness, color, and audio.

Note: LucidWave is for wellness and self-care. It is not a medical device and does not diagnose, treat, cure, or prevent disease.

Designed for comfort

  • Choose light color: white, amber, red, or green
  • Timer presets (5/15/30 min) + custom minutes
  • Sound only, light only, or both
  • Background audio support

Made with love

My mom was brilliant—funny, sharp, unstoppable. Watching her memory fade was heartbreaking. LucidWave is my way of honoring her and helping others explore promising brain health practices.

Safety first

  • If you have photosensitive epilepsy, consult a clinician first.
  • Start gently: lower brightness, shorter sessions.
  • Stop if you feel discomfort.
